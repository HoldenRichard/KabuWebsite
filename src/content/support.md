# Kabu Support

Need help? Email **learnkabu@gmail.com** and we will get back to you within 2 to 3 days. Kabu is built by a small team, so every message gets read by a person.

## Common questions

**Is the money in Kabu real?**
No. Everything in Kabu is simulated. You practice with virtual cash against real market history. No real money can be deposited, withdrawn, invested, or lost.

**Do I need money to use Kabu?**
No. The full 25 lesson curriculum is free, and so is everything you need to learn. Premium adds extras, never required learning.

**How do unlocks work?**
Kabu opens features as you learn. Historical scenarios unlock when you finish Unit 3, and paper trading unlocks when you finish Unit 4. This is by design: you practice after you have the tools to understand what you are practicing.

**How do I cancel Premium?**
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, choose Kabu, and tap Cancel. You keep Premium until the end of the period you paid for.

**How do I delete my account?**
In Kabu, go to Settings and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all of your data. If anything goes wrong, email us and we will handle it.

**Is Kabu financial advice?**
No. Kabu teaches general concepts about how markets work, using simulated practice. Nothing in Kabu is investment advice, a recommendation to buy or sell anything, or a prediction about real markets.
