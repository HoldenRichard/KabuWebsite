# Kabu Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-23

## The short version

Kabu is a financial education app. Everything you trade in Kabu is simulated. We collect what we need to run your account and improve the app, and nothing more. We do not track you across other apps, we do not show ads, and we never sell your data.

## What we collect

**Your account.** When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and, if you share it, your name. We use these to create and secure your account.

**A user ID.** Your account gets an internal identifier. It links your progress to you so your learning survives a new phone.

**Your progress and practice.** Lessons completed, quiz results, XP, streaks, simulated trades, and practice portfolios. This is the point of the app. All trading data is simulated. No real money, holdings, or brokerage information ever touches Kabu.

**Usage analytics.** We collect anonymous-style usage events, such as which lesson was completed or which scenario was played, through Google Firebase. These help us see what is working and what is confusing. They are not used to track you across other companies' apps or websites, and we never put personal details inside them.

**Updates list, only if you ask.** If you check the box asking to hear about new features, we keep your email on a list for that purpose. You can leave the list any time by contacting us.

## What we do not do

We do not show ads. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone. We do not use your data for advertising across other apps or websites. We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or anything from outside the app.

## Where your data lives

Kabu runs on Google Firebase, which stores your account and progress on our behalf. Google acts as a processor under its own privacy and security commitments. Your data is protected in transit and at rest.

## Your choices

**Delete your account.** Open Settings inside Kabu and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all data connected to it. You can also email us and we will do it for you.

**Leave the updates list.** Email us and we will remove you.

## Children

Kabu is a general audience app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the date at the top. Meaningful changes will be called out in the app.
